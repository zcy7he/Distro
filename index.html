This is awesome but
how does it work?

First we register the audio signature.

When you upload a piece of audio , it is tagged with a unique cryptographic signature. A DistroToken corresponding to this unique ID is then created.

The second is our DistroToken crawler.

We've built a complex web-crawler that compares audio found to registered DistroToken tokens. If a piece of audio is found that contains material that has been registered in the DistroToken system , we will work to recover the royalties. We crawl all major media platforms and are constantly expanding our reach as we grow.
